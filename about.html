Introduction

Reese is a female black American Labrador Retriever. She is fully grown and weighs about 55 pounds! She currently lives in Maryland with her parents.

2

Birthday

Reese was born on July 20th, 2018 in Smithtown, New York. Her litter was named after candy so she has siblings with names like Hershey, Pez, Twizzler, and Starburst.

3

Growing Up

Reese was in training to be a guide dog for Guide Dog Foundation for the Blind! She began her training on October 30, 2018 at the University of Maryland but was released and adopted on July 26th, 2019 due to medical reasons.

4

Fun Facts!

Reese's favorite treats are carrots and peanut butter filled Kongs, but she will eat about anything if she could. She also loves to swim, chew on sticks, and play with other dogs!
